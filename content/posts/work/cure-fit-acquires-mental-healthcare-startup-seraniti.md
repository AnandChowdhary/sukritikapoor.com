Bengaluru-based health and fitness startup Cure.fit has acquired mental healthcare startup Seraniti for an undisclosed amount. Seraniti has rebranded to Mind.fit and its team of 15 therapists has been absorbed post acquisition. Founded in 2016 by Myntra Co-founder Mukesh Bansal and former Flipkart executive Ankit Nagori, Cure.fit is backed by Hrithik Roshan and Flipkart CEO Kalyan Krishnamurthy.
