Bali’s Ayana Resort and Spa has banned all electronic gadgets, including smartphones, cameras and tablets, near one of its swimming pools. This is part of the resort’s ‘In the Moment’ campaign that encourages guests to relax by playing games, reading books or swimming. “Our intention is to preserve who we are by valuing human connection and me-time,” the resort said.
