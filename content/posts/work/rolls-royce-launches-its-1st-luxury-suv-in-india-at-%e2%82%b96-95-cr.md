British luxury automaker Rolls-Royce has launched its first luxury SUV Cullinan in India at an ex-showroom price of ₹6.95 crore. Powered by a 6.75-litre V12 engine, Cullinan has a top speed of 250 kmph. It also has a drinks cabinet with Rolls-Royce whisky glasses, a decanter, champagne flutes and refrigerator built into a fixed centre console between the rear seats.
