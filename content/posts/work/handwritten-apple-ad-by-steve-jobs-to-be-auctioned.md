A handwritten advertisement by Steve Jobs, offering a circuit board and manual for Apple’s first computer for $75, is set for auction on December 5 in New York. Jobs described the offer as “a real deal”. The manuscript is accompanied by two Polaroid photographs of the Apple-1 computer and its screen with Jobs’ address and phone number at the bottom.
