The prehistoric world is vast, but *Mega Bites Dinosaurs* breaks it all down into manageable chunks of information on need-to-know topics like dino diet, species identification, fossilization, and life after dinosaurs. Illustrations and photographs accent nearly every page, while highlighted text calls out important takeaways about each chapter topic, revealing new details about the amazing creatures that once roamed Earth. I worked on the 2020 revised edition of this book, updated with all the latest theories of paleontologists and other scientists around the globe in the ever-changing world of dinosaurs.
