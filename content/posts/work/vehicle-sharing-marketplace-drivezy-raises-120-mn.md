Bengaluru-based vehicle sharing marketplace Drivezy has raised $20 million in a Series B funding round led by Das Capital and $100 million in asset financing, to be handled by existing investor Anypay. Yamaha Motor, Axan Partners and IT-Farm also participated in the funding round. Founded in 2015, Drivezy lets vehicle owners list their idle vehicles and rent them to customers.
