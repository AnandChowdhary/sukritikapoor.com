Google has invested in Japanese artificial intelligence and machine learning startup ABEJA as part of its Series C round, bringing the total funding raised by ABEJA to over $53 million to date. ABEJA offers machine learning solutions for data gathering, annotation and analysis. A year ago, Google opened an AI and machine learning lab in China’s Beijing.
