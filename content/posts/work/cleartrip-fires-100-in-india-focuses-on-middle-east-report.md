Online booking platform Cleartrip has fired nearly 100 employees in India and is shifting its focus to the Middle East, according to a report. Cleartrip reportedly fired employees across its offices in Gurugram, Mumbai and Bengaluru in functions like business contracting and business development. The move was due to Cleartrip’s declining hotel and flight bookings in India, the report added.
