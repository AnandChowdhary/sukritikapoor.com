Homegrown co-working startup 91springboard has raised $10.2 million in a funding round led by Singapore-based marketing technology company FreakOut and Japan’s Shinsei Bank. The latest round of funding takes 91springboard’s total funding raised till date to around $33 million. The startup was launched in 2013 and is currently operational in nine cities across the country.
