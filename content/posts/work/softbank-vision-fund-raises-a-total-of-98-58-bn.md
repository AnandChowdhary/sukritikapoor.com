SoftBank Vision Fund has raised a total of $98.58 billion from 14 investors since the date of the first sale on May 20, 2017, according to a recent filing. According to last year’s annual filing, the fund had raised $93.15 billion from 8 investors. This year, the fund has added six new investors and raised $5.43 billion.
