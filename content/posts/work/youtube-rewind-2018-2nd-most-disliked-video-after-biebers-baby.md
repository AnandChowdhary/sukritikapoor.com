YouTube Rewind 2018 has become the second-most disliked video on the platform with 8.1 million dislikes after Justin Bieber’s ‘Baby’, leading with 9.7 million dislikes. The video was released last week and is the most disliked video of the year. It stars Will Smith and YouTubers like Liza Koshy, Marques Brownlee and Bhuvan Bam and includes K-Pop and Fortnite references.
