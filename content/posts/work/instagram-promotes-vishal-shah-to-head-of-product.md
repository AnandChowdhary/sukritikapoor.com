Instagram on Monday announced Vishal Shah’s promotion to Head of Product, effective immediately. Shah assumed the position previously held by Adam Mosseri, who was appointed as Instagram’s head in October. Shah had joined Instagram in 2015 as the Director of Product Management where he headed consumer, business, and revenue products divisions, including search, explore, Ads, IGTV and shopping.
