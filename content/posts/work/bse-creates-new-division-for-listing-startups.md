The Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) on Wednesday announced the creation of a new division within its small and medium enterprises (SMEs) segment to list startups. Startups seeking listing should have existed for at least two years at the time of filing. Further, they should not have been referred to National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) under Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code.
