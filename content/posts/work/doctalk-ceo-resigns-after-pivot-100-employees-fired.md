Mumbai-based health-tech startup DocTalk’s Co-founder and CEO Akshat Goenka resigned in August after it changed its business model and fired nearly 100 employees in 2018.  “It was the right time to take some time off from work… Good… for the team to restructure,” Goenka said. Founded in 2016, DocTalk enables communication between doctors and patients with separate interfaces for both.
