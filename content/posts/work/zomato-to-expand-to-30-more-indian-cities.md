Foodtech startup Zomato has announced that it will expand to 30 more Indian cities including Puducherry, Jamshedpur, Ambala and Meerut over the next week. It is also looking to hire nearly 5,000 delivery partners in these cities. “We have been surprised by the demand in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities,” Mohit Gupta, Food Delivery CEO at Zomato, said.
