With clear, concise coverage of all the core curriculum physics topics, *Super Simple Physics*, another book in the Super Simple series is the perfect accessible supplementary guide to physics for students. Breaking down the information into easy, manageable chunks, this book covers everything from atoms to astronomy and forces to flotation. Each topic is fully illustrated, to support the information, make the facts crystal clear, and bring the science to life. For key ideas, a “How it works” panel explains the theory with the help of bright, simple graphics. And for revision, a handy “Key facts” box provides a simple summary readers can check back on later.

I worked on this book from start to finish – conducting picture research for briefs, planning spread layouts with designers and carrying out editorial sense checks, editing author text for the target audience, placing final text in the layouts, and liaising with UK editors, and external consultants, indexers and proofreaders to make the finished book.
