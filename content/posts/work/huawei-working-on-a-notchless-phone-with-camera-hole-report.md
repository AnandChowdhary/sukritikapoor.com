Huawei is reportedly working on a notchless smartphone with a circular front camera cutout in the top left corner of the screen. The design is similar to Samsung Galaxy A8s which is also reportedly a notchless smartphone based on Samsung’s Infinity-O display technology, set to reportedly launch in December. Huawei could also release the phone in December, as per reports.
