Online classifieds platform OLX India on Monday announced the offline expansion of its used-car business in a joint venture with Germany’s Frontier Car Group (FCG). The venture called ‘Cash My Car’ marks FCG’s entry into the Indian market. “Our plan is to expand OLX Cash My Car model in top 40 cities in India with…150 outlets by 2021,” OLX said.
